I'm building a personal workout-logging app. It greets me with ONE short line, credited to its
source. I want a rich, varied mix — sometimes a friend hyping me up, sometimes real wisdom to
sit with.

I need TWO sets, because the app speaks to me at two moments:

  1. BEFORE  — I just opened the app, haven't logged anything yet. The "let's do this" moment.
               Get me off the couch and into it.
  2. AFTER   — I just finished logging my workout. The "good job" moment. Make me feel rewarded,
               proud, seen.

Write me 20 phrases for EACH set (40 total).

THE 50 / 50 SPLIT — this is important. Within EACH set, make it roughly:
  • HALF "hype coach"  — YOUR OWN original words. A hype friend / coach in my corner / teammate
    on the sideline. Warm, alive, personal. Uses "you" / "let's" / "we." Makes me FEEL something.
  • HALF "wise words"  — a mix of:
       - YOUR OWN original wise/reflective lines (calm truths about effort, patience, showing up),
         AND
       - REAL existing quotes about effort, discipline, sport, or perseverance — from athletes,
         coaches, philosophers, or known figures. These must be genuine quotes, attributed to the
         real person. Do NOT invent a fake quote and slap a famous name on it. If you're not sure
         a quote is real, use your own words instead and leave the author blank.

THE FEEL:
- Hype lines: pumped, fired up, rooting for me, celebratory.
- Wise lines: grounding, thoughtful, the kind of thing I'd reread on a hard day.
- I keep getting BLAND, emotionless filler from AIs ("Consistency is key.") — for the hype half,
  DO NOT do that. The wise half can be calm, but still has to mean something.
- BEFORE = anticipation, momentum, "go get it."  AFTER = pride, reward, "you did it."

RULES:
- Each phrase is one line, max ~70 characters. Punchy enough to read at a glance.
- No emojis, no hashtags, no quotation marks around the phrase text itself.
- Originality for your own lines; accuracy for real quotes.

OUTPUT FORMAT — return ONLY this JSON object, nothing else.
Each entry is an object:
  - "text"   : the phrase
  - "kind"   : "hype" or "wise"
  - "author" : the real person for a genuine quote, or null if it's your own words
